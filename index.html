Maletsunyane

The imposing Maletsunyane Falls,which,with a gigantic and unique 200-metre drop, thunderously cascade down from the heights of the Thaba Putsoa Mountains.

Tsehlanyane National Park

Tucked up in the nouth of the country in the majastic Maloti mountains,Tsehlanyane National Park is a great place to spend a few days in Lesotho's great outdoors.With great hiking, pony trekking and beautiful mountain scenery

Thaba Bosiu

Located 24km outside of the city of Maseru. Thaba Bosiu is a National Monument and Lesotho's foremost tourist destination holding great historical significance as the birthplace of the Basothi People

Mohale Dam

A very large rockfill dam, located on the Senqunyane River. Mohale offers a variety of activities such as boat cruise, pony trakking and hiking.
